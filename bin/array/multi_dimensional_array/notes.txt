Multidimensional arrays are arrays of arrays with each element of the array holding the reference of other array. 
These are also known as Jagged Arrays.

Multidimensional Arrays can be defined in simple words as array of arrays. 
Data in multidimensional arrays are stored in tabular form (in row major order).

Three – dimensional array is a complex form of a multidimensional array. A three – dimensional array can be seen as an array
of two – dimensional array for easier understanding.
